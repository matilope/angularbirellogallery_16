About us

Birello Gallery is an emerging familiar business searching for the best products thinking in quality, value and exquisiteness. The products we offer not only delight customers regarding decorative value but also considering art itself as an investment for the future.

Returning identity to unappraised artworks

Birello gallery is run by Ignacio Birello, born in Buenos Aires during 1997. He is one of the youngest art dealers in the world, having studied business administration at UBA and art valuation at ESNECA. Without any parental background in the field of art and antiques, he focuses primarily on european artworks from the 16th century until the 19th century. Despite beginning to collect and deal with french porcelain, his passion for paintings is undisputed. Focusing on South American collections due to immigration waves from the prior centuries, he often reaches artworks to the European market either as an anonymous seller or representing Birello Gallery.
